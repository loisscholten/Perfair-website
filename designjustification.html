User Testing Report

After our prototypes on Adobe XD, we went on having an user testing that had the following tasks:

  • Search "about us" to learn more about Perfair's mission and vision.
  • Contact us through the contact page via email, phone number or our social media platforms.
  • Click on one of our social media buttons.

The main issues we had encountered with our prototype were crucial to our success for an easy and interesting flow to get around our website. One of which is that some pages weren't linked to each other, another is that the page was not structured correctly. Information were down below and may lose user's interest quickly.

Improvements:

  • Update all pages to be linked with each other.
  • Structurize the pages correctly.
  • Easy navigatitions all around.
